IoT Security Checklist

  • Default usernames and passwords are publicly known and can be easily exploited by attackers. Change them immediately when setting up a new device.

  • 2FA adds a second layer of security, ensuring that even if someone knows your password, they can’t access your devices without a second code.

  • Place IoT devices on a separate network from personal devices to limit the damage if one device is compromised.

  • Manufacturers release firmware updates to patch security holes. Check your devices often and apply updates when available.
